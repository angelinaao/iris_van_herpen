JAPANESE AIRFABRIC
NOOSPHERE DRESS
[SYMPOIESIS AW25]
Van Herpen used Japanese airfabric and translucent carbon-fibre bonings for dresses that reference Turritopsis dohrnii, a immortal jellyfish.
She also used blue and greenocean-like colours for the dramatic Noosphere look, which was made from organza stretched over multiple and various carbon-fibre bonings.
